Alpha stage comes after discovery and during this phase we will:

  • build prototypes of the service
  • test prototypes with real users
  • test hypothesis and assumptions
  • determine if the service can be technically built

Alpha is also the time to identify any risks for the beta phase as soon as possible.

It will also be possible to identify how the service metrics can be measured. For example, the start and end point, potential drop-offs or areas where a user may need to go away and do something.

By building prototypes, it should also be possible to identify any problems with the design of the service and solve them early on.

By the end of alpha, we should:

  • know whether to move into beta
  • what needs to be built in the beta phase
  • know what to build in the beta phase
