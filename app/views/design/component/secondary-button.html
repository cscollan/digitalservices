Component

An extention of the govuk-button which does not use the green styling.

Example

When to use

Consider how a service can be designed, to avoid using secondary buttons. There are instances when a secondary button may be needed, for example:

  • where a page has equally weighted actions but one is the preferred option
  • admin interfaces where there may be multiple actions applicable

User research

We do not have any evidence that secondary buttons cause problems for users. However, their styling may resemble a disabled button and confuse users.

It is important to give buttons descriptive actions and not just "Click here". The button should inform the user of what will happen. For example, "Save and continue"
